Private Pilot Licence

Let aside the ground exams that most students self study before taking the tests on site, the obtention of a PPL(H) requires a minimum of 45 hrs of flight training, including 10 hrs of solo. The duration varies depending on commitment.

Learn More About PRIVATE PILOT LICENCE

Commercial Pilot Licence

Holding a Commercial Pilot Licence, CPL(H) gives you the status of professional helicopter pilot. To achieve this, 155 hrs of flying time post licence is required, of which 50 hrs must be Pilot In Command (PIC).

Learn More About COMMERCIAL PILOT LICENCE

Type Rating

Having achieved your PPL(H), you may wish to fly different types of helicopter. A type specific ground training course followed by a minimum of 5 Hrs of flight training will suffice to put you to the Type Rating test.

Learn More About TYPE RATING

Night Rating

Holding a night rating gives you the most flexibility, as sunset will no longer prevent you from flying. To achieve this, 100 hrs of flying post licence is required, of which 60 Hrs must be Pilot In Command.

Learn More About NIGHT RATING

Self-Fly Hire

With an impressive fleet of over 30 helicopters, HQ will certainly be able to facilitate your flying requirements, either on a short term hiring or on a long term leasing basis.

Learn More About SELF-FLY HIRE
