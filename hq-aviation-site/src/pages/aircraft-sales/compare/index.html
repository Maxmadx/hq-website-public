Which Model is Right for You?

R22 - Training Excellence

The ideal choice for flight training and building hours economically. Low operating costs make it the world's most popular training helicopter. Perfect first helicopter for those learning to fly.

Best for: PPL training, hour building, budget-conscious flying

R44 - Versatile Performer

The world's best-selling civil helicopter offers the perfect balance of capability, comfort, and value. Four seats make it ideal for private owners wanting to travel with family or friends.

Best for: Private ownership, family travel, business trips

R66 - Turbine Capability

Turbine reliability meets Robinson efficiency. Five seats, superior hot & high performance, and the new NxG glass cockpit make the R66 the ultimate owner-pilot helicopter.

Best for: Premium owners, business use, demanding environments

R88 - Commercial Excellence

Robinson's flagship twin-turbine helicopter opens new possibilities for operators requiring capacity, redundancy, and premium features. The next chapter in Robinson aviation.

Best for: Commercial operators, corporate transport, charter
