4

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

No experience is necessary. Trial lessons are designed for complete beginners. Your instructor will guide you through everything step by step.
Minimum age is 14 years. You need to be able to reach the controls comfortably. If you have any health concerns, please discuss with us when booking. Most common conditions are not an issue for trial lessons.
Friends and family are welcome to watch from our clubhouse. Some of our helicopters have rear seats - please ask when booking if you'd like a passenger to join the flight.
We need reasonable visibility for training flights. If weather prevents your flight, we'll reschedule at no extra cost. We'll call you the day before if there are any concerns.
Yes! Your trial lesson is recorded in a flight logbook and counts towards the flight hours required for a Private Pilot License (PPL).
