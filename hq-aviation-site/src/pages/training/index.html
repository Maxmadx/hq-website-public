Training FAQ

The minimum flight time is 45 hours. Most students complete within 3-6 months, depending on frequency of lessons and weather conditions.
A Class 2 medical certificate is required for PPL training. This can be obtained from a CAA approved aviation medical examiner (AME).
Helicopter training is conducted in visual flight conditions. Your instructor will advise on suitable weather days.
