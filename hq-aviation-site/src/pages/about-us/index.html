Our Story

A Legacy of Aviation Excellence

Founded by world-renowned helicopter pilot Quentin Smith, HQ Aviation has grown from a passion for flight into the UK's premier Robinson Helicopter dealership.

Based at Denham Aerodrome, just outside London, we offer comprehensive aviation services including aircraft sales, flight training, maintenance, and extraordinary expedition experiences to some of the world's most remote destinations.

Our team combines decades of experience with a genuine love for helicopters. Whether you're taking your first trial flight or purchasing a new aircraft, we're dedicated to providing exceptional service and expert guidance.
