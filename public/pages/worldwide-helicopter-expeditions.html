Fly the World with HQ Aviation

Feeling adventurous? Let's reveal the explorer in you.

HQ organises great expeditions throughout the year & can tailor expeditions to your own requirements.

We have conducted missions ranging in difficulty and duration across the entire globe. We have taken HQ’ers through the stunning landscapes of:

Costa Rica, Norway, Iceland, Greece, the Faroe Islands, Sardinia, Ireland, Corsica, Scotland, Italy, The Swiss Alps, Austria, Wales, Germany, Denmark, Sweden, France, Belgium, Croatia, Luxembourg, Slovenia & the Sahara! We've also flown out to exotic locations and brought other companies on Expeditions - around Russia, China, Siberia and Alaska!
