R88

The Robinson R88 stands as the flagship of the fleet, a heavy-lift helicopter powered by the formidable Safran Arriel 2W engine. Breaking new ground for the manufacturer, this ten-seat aircraft delivers industrial-grade performance for offshore transport and EMS, yet remains uniquely accessible for the private owner. Its cavernous cabin offers a true "flying SUV" experience, easily accommodating large families, luggage, and recreational gear with room to spare. The R88 launch also introduces the 'Serenity' program—a comprehensive concierge support package where owners pay a fixed hourly rate that covers all scheduled and unscheduled maintenance, parts, and labor. There are no surprise bills when a component times out, ensuring a turnkey ownership experience whether flying for business, utility, or pure leisure.
