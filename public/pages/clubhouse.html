The Clubhouse

Where pilots come together.

The HQ Aviation Clubhouse is a community hub for helicopter enthusiasts. Whether you're a student pilot, seasoned aviator, or simply passionate about rotary flight, you'll find a welcoming space to connect, learn, and share experiences.

Facilities

Pilot Lounge

Comfortable space to relax before and after flights, with refreshments and flight planning facilities.

Library

Extensive aviation library, current charts, and study materials for all ratings.

Events

Regular fly-ins, guest speakers, safety seminars, and social gatherings.

Maintenance Viewing

Watch our engineers at work and learn about helicopter maintenance.

Networking

Connect with fellow pilots, instructors, and industry professionals.

Ground School

Dedicated classrooms for theoretical training and exam preparation.
