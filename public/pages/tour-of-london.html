Experience London from Above

As a way to familiarize yourself with helicopters, take to the skies over one of the most impressive skylines in the world, right in our neighbourhood. With our airfield located within Greater London, we are just a short hop from a beautiful skyline and an unforgettable flight experience. Spanning the length of London from East to West and back again, you'll be able to observe in stunning detail the whole of London, from its historic landmarks to its modern marvels, all from the unique vantage point of our Robinson R66 Turbine helicopter.
