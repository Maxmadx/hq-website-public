NEW AIRCRAFT

HQ Aviation is built on a dynasty of rotorcraft excellence, tracing its roots to 1986 when former naval pilot Mike Smith established his first Robinson dealership, laying the groundwork for the UK’s Robinson market. This legacy is driven forward by his son, Captain Quentin Smith ("Captain Q")—a two-time World Freestyle Helicopter Champion and the first person to pilot a piston helicopter to both the North and South Poles. Based at Denham Aerodrome, HQ Aviation. When you buy from us, you aren't just purchasing a helicopter; you are acquiring an aircraft backed by a team that has tested Robinson engineering to the ends of the earth.
